# Appendix D: Protected Space Decision Tree

*When a Red Alert sounds, choose the highest-priority option you can reach.*

!!! danger "Warning"
    **WHEN TO LEAVE:** Stay for **10 minutes OR until all-clear** — whichever comes later. **Siren duration = defense time.** Border areas may have 15 seconds or less.

### Priority List (choose the highest you can reach)

1. **PRIORITY 1: MAMAD (Apartment Protected Space)**
   - Close blast door (handle 90°)
   - Close steel + glass windows
   - Sit against inner wall
   - WAIT FOR ALL-CLEAR

2. **PRIORITY 2: SHELTER (Building or Public Shelter)**
   - Enter building or public shelter
   - Close the door
   - WAIT FOR ALL-CLEAR

3. **PRIORITY 3: INNER STAIRWELL (No Windows)**
   - Centre floor — not top, not ground
   - Stay on staircase, not hallway
   - WAIT FOR ALL-CLEAR

4. **PRIORITY 4: INNER ROOM (Maximum Walls, Minimum Windows)**
   - Inner wall, below window line
   - Not facing door. Close all doors
   - WAIT FOR ALL-CLEAR

5. **PRIORITY 5: LAST RESORT (Nothing Else Available)**
   - Lie flat on the ground
   - Protect head with hands
   - WAIT FOR ALL-CLEAR

**NOT valid shelter locations:** Kitchen · Bathroom · Toilet · Building entrance lobby · Caravans / prefab · Areas with ceramics, porcelain, or glass

**Stairwell positioning:** 3+ floors: at least 2 floors above you. 3-storey building: middle floor only.
