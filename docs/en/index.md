# Israel Wartime Readiness Field Guide

**Field Guide & Checklists — V4**

*By Daniel Rosehill + Claude Opus | 20 March 2026*

A comprehensive civilian preparedness field guide for wartime in Israel, based on Home Front Command (Pikud HaOref) guidance. This guide provides printable checklists, situational procedures, and practical guidance for keeping you and your household safe during emergencies.
