## Terrorist Infiltration - Life-Saving Guidelines

How should one act during an event of a terrorist infiltration?

Immediately enter a building, lock the door, and turn the lights on. Enter the protected space, close the door properly, and sit below the window line. Do not exit the protected space until you receive a notification from the official authorities that the event has ended.  
[For full guidelines](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/life-saving-guidelines/terrorist-infiltration/ "חדירת מחבלים - הנחיות מצילות חיים")

How should one act if you are outdoors during a terrorist infiltration?

Find a sheltered space and stay there until you receive notification from the official authorities that the event has ended.

What should I do after being secluded in the protected space during an infiltration of terrorists’ event?

Immediately report the situation to the area security officer, the neighbors, the Israel Police, the IDF or the local municipality.

What should I do during the infiltration of Terrorists while I am driving?

Continue driving until you reach a safe place.

Can the door handle of the protected room (Mamad) be removed in order to prevent hostile factors from entering the protected space?

The door to the MAMAD (Residential Protected Room) is built to be open during an emergency. Removing the handle harms the mechanism and function of the door and its effectiveness in protecting human life. In order to block entry from the outside, more effective and simple means can be used that prevent the door from opening without damaging the door's functionality, such as using a heavy object.

Should I sit on the floor during an event of a terrorist infiltration?

It is recommended that you do. Terrorists seek to kill. Movement or shadows inside homes may draw their attention to the presence of people in the house.

Can social networks be used to report my location during an event of a terrorist infiltration?

Avoid sharing your location on social networks and various media.

Do I have to turn off the TV, radio and any other device that makes noise during a Terrorist Infiltration Event?

Yes. Terrorists seek to kill. Lights and noises can draw their attention to the presence of people in the house. Therefore, make sure that during the infiltration of Terrorists, the house is dark and no noise will be heard from it.

Should I remain quiet and not talk during an event of a terrorist infiltration?

Yes. Terrorists seek to kill. Lights and noises can draw their attention to the presence of people in the house. Therefore, make sure that during the infiltration of Terrorists, the house is dark and no noise will be heard from it.

How do we know when the Terrorist Infiltration Event has ended?

The Security Forces will announce the end of the event.

When can you exit the protected space during an event of a terrorist infiltration?

You can exit the protected space only after receiving notification from the official authorities.

I have a weapon. How should I act when receiving a Terrorist Infiltration Event Alert?

During a Terrorist Infiltration Event, stay indoors, lock windows and doors and turn off lights. Security officials are handling the event. If there is a licensed weapon and you have the skill required to using it, you can aim in the direction of the front door. Fire the weapon only when the conditions for opening fire allow it and after you have identified that it is indeed a terrorist, in order to prevent misfire towards the security forces.

What to do if the children are crying and making noise during an event of a terrorist infiltration?

Use a calm and quiet tone to reassure the children, using the message saying: "The security forces are nearby and are handling the situation. Do not worry, we are protected at home."

We have received a Terrorist Infiltration Alert and we hear gunshots or voices speaking in Arabic near the house. How should I act?

Continue to stay secluded inside the protected space with the doors and windows closed, do not turn on the light, and report to the neighbors, local security officer, the Israeli Police, IDF, and the local municipality.

How to act if there is an alert for rocket and missile attack during an event of a terrorist infiltration?

Do not go to a protected space outside the house, including the staircase. Remain in the protected space you’re in.
