## Emergency Training for Adults

How to coordinate emergency training for adults?

To coordinate emergency training, you can contact the Home Front Command through the information center at [104](tel: 104), or by sending a text message to [052-9104104](tel: 0529104104), or **by contacting** us in this website, and provide the following information: name of institution , institution address, name of contact person, contact phone number, target audience and number of participants.

How long does the emergency training for adults last?

The emergency training last about 45 minutes.

Who can book emergency training for adults?

The Home Front Command, as part of its activities to prepare Israeli citizens for emergencies, provides guidance on emergency preparedness to all citizens. Every citizen and every institution in Israel, is entitled to book the training.

Who delivers the emergency training content for adults?

The training is provided by the Home Front Command's emergency instructors.

Where can the adult training sessions be held?

The training takes place at the place that was determined by the person who booked the training.

What is the minimum number of participants required in order to hold the adult emergency training?

In order to conduct the training, a minimum of 10 participants must be included.

What does the adult emergency training include?

• Preparation for missile and rocket attack - including Home Front Command alert measures, life-saving guidelines during a rocket and missile attack , and preparation of the protected space.  
• Preparation for an Earthquake - including life-saving guidelines during an earthquake, and earthquake preparedness measures.  
• General emergency preparedness - including the importance of preparing for emergencies and steps to prepare the home and family for emergency.
