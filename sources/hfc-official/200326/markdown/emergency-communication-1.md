## A list of important telephone numbers to prepare in advance

When [preparing your home](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/info/preparing-protected-space/1201/ "ציוד לשעת חירום") and the supplies needed for emergencies, it’s very important to draw up a list with the telephone numbers of emergency organizations, relatives, and neighbors.

Past experience teaches us that during emergency and stressful situations, it is important to get the relevant emergency organizations, the local municipality and others that can assist you such as family and friends, involved as soon as possible.

Do not rely on your phone’s records - Draw up a written list with telephone numbers, place the list in a visible location along with your emergency supplies, and ensure that there are enough copies for each of your family members.

Additionally, when noting relatives, friends, and acquaintances, it’s important to mention added benefits if any (for example: David - medic, Neighbor - plumber, Acquaintance - electrician, etc.)

### The importance of preparing a list of telephone numbers

Past experience teaches us that it is important to involve the relevant emergency organizations or people who are not under the same type of danger as soon as possible. This is for a number of reasons:

• Time is critical in emergencies - the rapid arrival of medical assistance will improve the condition of wounded people, and the rapid arrival of a fire squad will mean the fire is extinguished quicker.

• Remote assistance - Emergency rescue forces may be able to provide critical guidance from afar - for example, providing assistance to the injured. Nonetheless, it is always best to be prepared to cope independently with possible scenarios.

• Emergency incidents can develop in unknown ways. Situations may deteriorate rapidly and you may be unable to contact emergency and rescue forces within a short period of time.

## Q&A

Emergency Equipment and Communications

Emergency Preparedness

What is the right equipment to prepare for an emergency?

It is important to prepare in advance the necessary equipment that will assist us and our household members during an emergency, and to store it inside a bag that we can take with us when needed.  
For the emergency equipment list, [click here](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/info/preparing-protected-space/1201/ "נערכים למצבי חירום עם כל הציוד הנדרש - איראן 7").

What emergencies require to prepare equipment ahead of time?

In emergency situations that require the population to remain in lockdown for an extended period of time, in situations where it is necessary to evacuate the house, and in situations in whichf damage to infrastructure may damage the fabric of life. A mass casualty incident also requires preparing equipment in advance because a large number of cases will force the rescue forces to set priorities for handling cases and will extend the time of arrival to each home and each patient.

What are the phone numbers of call centers that provide emotional support and help during emergencies?

[For a list of phone number for call centers that provide emotional support and help](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/info/emergency-management/1307/ "חשים מתח? עצות להתמודדות").

Why is it important to prepare emergency phone numbers in advance?

Past experience shows that in emergency and distress situations, it is important to involve the emergency entities, the local municipality and other factors such as family members, acquaintances and neighbors, who can help as soon as possible. This is due to the following reasons:  

• Time in an emergency event is critical - in emergency and distress situations, it is important to involve relevant people and entities as soon as possible. A quick arrival of the emergency forces will help in a life-saving response. For example, a quick arrival of the fire and rescue forces will allow to rescue trapped people and put out the fire faster, and a quick arrival of medical forces will make it possible to provide first aid quickly, which can save lives.  
• Remote assistance - the emergency factors can provide essential guidance from a distance, for example in providing assistance to the injured until the arrival of the ambulance and paramedics. However, it is always better to be prepared to deal independently with possible scenarios.  
• The development of emergency events is unknown - during an emergency, there may be situations in which we will not be able to contact the emergency and rescue forces within a short time. That is why it is important to prepare in advance a phone list of family members, acquaintances and neighbors who can help in an emergency.

Why should water be prepared in emergency equipment?

There are emergency situations in which the regular water supply may be affected, so it is important to prepare water in the amount of three liters per person per day, for three days.
