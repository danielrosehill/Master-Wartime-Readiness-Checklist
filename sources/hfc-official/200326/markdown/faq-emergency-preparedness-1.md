## Emergency Preparedness

What is the role of the Home Front Command?

The Home Front Command is entrusted with civil defense issues in routine and emergency situations.  
On normal days, the Home Front Command operates in order to guide the population on how to behave in emergency situations, instructs and directs the local municipalities, government ministries and infrastructure entities to carry out their duties in an emergency in the fields of civil defense.  
During an emergency, the Home Front Command activates the Search and Rescue Unit, provides alerts to the residents in the face of a threat, instructs the population on how to act, guides the local municipalities and government ministries in performing their duties in an emergency in the fields of civil defense.  
Along with the preparedness of the security forces to provide solutions to the various incidents, the citizens can prepare themselves and their family for emergency situations.

Why does the Home Front Command routinely publish emergency preparedness content?

Dangers and emergencies may strike us without time for advance preparation. The Home Front Command is responsible for the population's preparedness for an emergency and as such, provides information and tools to dealing with an emergency throughout the year. This comes out of the experience in Israel and around the world which proves that people who knew how to act in an emergency, were able to save their lives and the lives of their loved ones. Prepared in routine, protected in an emergency.

Does the Home Front Command conduct emergency preparedness training?

The Home Front Command regularly conducts training and guidance activities for the general public, organizations and educational institutions in how to prepare for the various emergency situations.  
[For more information on Home Front Command trainings](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/emergency-preparedness/emergency-education/2406/ "הדרכות לציבור הרחב").

Why is it important to prepare for emergencies in routine times?

Emergencies such as a rocket and missile attack or an earthquake, may strike us without time for advance preparation, so the best time to prepare is today, in routine.  
Preparing the population for emergency situations during routine times, is the most correct and effective way to better deal with emergency situations. From many studies conducted in Israel and around the world, we have learned that preparing the public for proper behavior in emergency situations improves coping. Beyond that, studies show that information reduces anxiety. The Home Front Command regularly conducts training and guidance activities for the public to evaluate various emergency situations.

How should you prepare for emergencies in routine times?

Preparing for emergency situations includes the following steps: knowing the guidelines for proper behavior when receiving a [rocket and missile attack alert](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/life-saving-guidelines/rocket-and-missile-attacks/ "ירי רקטות וטילים- מעודכן"), an [earthquake](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/life-saving-guidelines/earthquake/ "רעידת אדמה"), and a [hazardous materials incident](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/life-saving-guidelines/hazardous-materials-event/ "אירוע חומרים מסוכנים"); [Preparing the house](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/emergency-preparedness/preparing-routine/2104/ "הכנת הבית לחירום") - including choosing and preparing the protected space, clearing escape routes and handling objects that may endanger us on the walking path; and the [preparation of the family](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/emergency-preparedness/preparing-routine/2103/ "שיחה משפחתית על מצבי חירום") - in a way that will improve the ability to cope and increase the chances of being saved. For more information, go to the guide "[Preparing the family for emergencies](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/emergency-preparedness/preparing-routine/2102/ "תוכנית חירום משפחתית")" on this website.

What is the first action to take in preparing the house for an emergency?

Select the [protected space](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/info/preparing-protected-space/1200/ "הנחיות לבחירת המרחב המוגן") and prepare the equipment in it, as detailed [in this link.](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/info/preparing-protected-space/1201/ "ציוד לשעת חירום")

How do you prepare the house for an emergency?

After we have chosen a protected space in the house, objects in the protected space and on the way to it must be secured, so that they do not constitute an additional source of danger. Attach bookshelves and shelves to the walls, lock the cabinet doors, lock wheels on heavy appliances such as washing machines and refrigerators, place heavy objects in low places and try to identify additional risk points.  
[For a guide to preparing the home for emergencies](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/emergency-preparedness/preparing-routine/2104/ "הכנת הבית לחירום").

How do you prevent the formation of risk points at home?

Make sure the electricity, gas and fuel connections are in good condition, explain to the family members where the gas and electricity points are, and under what circumstances the faucets should be turned off (for example, a fire) and how. In addition, the way to the protected space must be cleared from objects, and heavy objects must be secured in the protected space and on the way to it in a way that they will not serve as an additional source of danger in an emergency.

What should be emphasized in practicing emergencies with family members?

The division of roles between family members should be verified, for example if new roles have been added. In addition, equipment such as water bottles and emergency lighting batteries must be refreshed. Make sure that each member of the family knows who to call in an emergency and that each member of the household has progressed in the tasks assigned to him, such as fixing objects or replacing equipment.  
[Preparing your family for emergency situations - guide](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/emergency-preparedness/preparing-routine/2103/ "שיחה משפחתית על מצבי חירום")

What are the steps of preparing a family emergency plan?

First, a [family conversation](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/emergency-preparedness/preparing-routine/2103/ "שיחה משפחתית על מצבי חירום") should be held to present the emergencies and ways to deal with them. Second, roles should be divided among family members. Finally, it is required to go through the various scenarios and ways in which we should act during each scenario. [Preparing Your Family for Emergency Situations](https://www.oref.org.il/eng/articles/emergency-preparedness/preparing-routine/2102/ "תוכנית חירום משפחתית").

How should we act if we are required to leave the house?

As part of the preparations, we must prepare an emergency bag with the necessary equipment. In case we have to leave the house immediately, the safest escape route must be planned. In this framework, doors and openings that are not in daily use such as back exits, balconies and emergency stairs must be taken into account. It is recommended to make sure that family members are familiar with these routes and that they are clear of hazards, moving objects that may block them, and flammable materials.

What roles can be assigned to young children as part of the family emergency preparedness?

The children can be involved in preparing the emergency equipment and deciding where to store it. In addition, they can be assigned small tasks to perform in an emergency, such as counting those present in the room and making sure everyone is present. In the preparation stages, the children can be involved in choosing the protected room. You can also appoint them as responsible for collecting items in the emergency equipment, for maintaining the emergency file (including tracking the expired items and replacing them), and for collecting games to be available in the protected space.
